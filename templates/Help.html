Need some Help?

Step 01

Open the Web Browser and Enter the Website's URL

Go to this link as shown above.



Step 02

Welcome to our Website

This is the Homepage of the website. And on the upper right side is the navigation bar which contains home, about us, help, and acknowledgement buttons.



Step 03

Uploading Video

Upon entering the website, the first thing you’ll see is the “Upload Your Video Here!” button. This button will direct you to the upload or drop files section where you can drop or select files from your device gallery or files.



Step 04

Choose a file/video to upload

From your files you may select a video, specifically videos of your cat that you desire to upload. Note that you can only select one video at a time for the process of in-heat detection. At the lower right of the window you’ll find “open” button, click to proceed to the upload.



Step 05

Video analysis and Validation

The website will then start analyzing the video being uploaded.

Step 06

Users' Feedback

1. The screen will pop up a small window asking a question, indicating that your female cat possibly took medication causing this behavior.


1.1 The screen will pop up a small window giving an instruction to take your cat’s rectal part for temperature checking using a thermometer. Follow the instructions and select an answer among buttons either “yes” or “no”. The analysis result and your validation response will lead you to the answer either your cat is in-heat or not.

Step 07

Results

Result window may display a positive/negative answer which state that “Your cat is ready to mate” and a negative answer “Your cat is not ready to mate”.



Step 08

Errors

On the screen also may display an error if you uploaded a wrong file, or empty file and video does not show any presence of the subject or specifically a female cat.



Step 09

About the page

Within the website, you may also find additional buttons such as about us, help, and acknowledgement page which you can explore to learn more about us.
